Special Educational Portal

This portal aims to provide accessible educational content for specially abled children.Special education is a type of instruction that helps students with disabilities learn the same skills as other students in school. It's based on the idea that all people have the right to learn and succeed, regardless of their disabilities. Special education is a type of formal education that helps people with learning difficulties, disabilities, or other challenges reach their full potential. Special education can help students with physical, cognitive, emotional, and behavioral challenges. The goal of special education is to provide students with the support and accommodations they need to lead fulfilling lives.
